# Fabric Finder - Monetization Strategies

## 💰 Revenue Streams

### 1. **Amazon Affiliate (Current - Primary)**
**Status:** Active
**Potential:** $500-5,000/month

**How it works:**
- 1-10% commission on Amazon purchases
- Average commission: 3-4% for clothing
- Need 3 sales within 180 days to keep PAAPI access

**Optimization:**
- Show Amazon products first (highest commission)
- A/B test match percentages to increase click-through
- Add "Free Prime shipping" badges
- Track which fabric types convert best

**Revenue Example:**
- 1,000 users/month
- 10% click-through = 100 clicks
- 5% conversion = 5 sales
- Average order: $80
- Commission (4%): $16
- **Monthly:** $80-400 (scales with traffic)
